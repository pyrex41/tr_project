ORDER REGARDING SANMINA CORPORATION'S
MOTIONS TO EXCLUDE EXPERT TESTIMONY
JANE J. BOYLE, United States Magistrate Judge.
*1  Before the Court is Plaintiff SANMINA
CORPORATION'S Motion to Exclude Expert Testimony
of David Gilchrist, Combined with Brief in Support
(the “Gilchrist Motion” ), Motion to Exclude Expert
Testimony of Sam Rhodes, Combined with Brief in Support
(the “Rhodes Motion” ), and Motion to Exclude Expert
Testimony of Richard Metters, Combined with Brief in
Support (the “Metters Motion” ). Having considered the
Motions, as well as any argument in support thereof and
in opposition thereto, as well as the Court's prior ruling in
Sanmina's Motion in Limine, the Court is of the opinion that
the following Order should be entered.
The Court hereby ORDERS as follows:
1. Upon withdrawal by BancTec of David Gilchrist as an
expert, the Gilchrist Motion is now moot;
2. The Rhodes Motion is denied; and,
3. The Metters Motion is denied.
