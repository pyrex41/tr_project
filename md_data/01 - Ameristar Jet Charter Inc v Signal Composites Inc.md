ORDER ON DEFENDANTS' MOTION TO STRIKE
AMERISTAR'S EXPERT WITNESSES
JANE J. BOYLE, District Judge.
*1  On August 9, 2001, came on to be heard, the
Defendants' Motion to Strike Ameristar's expert witnesses.
The Court, having reviewed the Defendants' motion, the
Plaintiff's response and the Defendants' reply, and having
heard arguments of counsel, finds that the Motion should be
denied in part; it is therefore,
ORDERED that the Defendants' Motion to Strike Ron
Kohlstrand is denied. It is also ORDERED that the
Defendants' Motion to Strike Dan Franceson is denied. With
regard to the Defendants' Motion to Strike Tom Woo's
testimony as an expert witness, the Court takes the matter
under advisement subject to the Plaintiff filing page and
line deposition excerpts of the portions of the Tom Woo
testimony that it intends to offer at trial on or before
October 5, 2001. The parties shall file memoranda addressing
whether or not the testimony is expert testimony or lay
witness testimony and whether or not the testimony should
be excluded under the principals established in Daubert v.
Merrell–Dow Pharmaceuticals, Inc., 509 U.S. 579, 113 S.Ct.
2786, 125 L.Ed.2d 469 (1993) on or before October 12, 2001.
A follow-up hearing to decide the Motion to Strike Woo's
testimony as an expert witness shall take place on October
25, 2001, at 2 o'clock p. m.
